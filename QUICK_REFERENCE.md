### Troubleshooting

**Button not visible?**

- Refresh the page
- Check browser console for errors

**Button in wrong position?**
- Hold and drag to reposition
- Position resets on page reload *(will be saved in future update)*

**Popup not opening?**
- Check browser console for errors
- Ensure extension has required permissions
- Try reloading the extension
